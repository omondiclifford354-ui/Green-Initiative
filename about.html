Who We Are

Green Initiative is a non-governmental organization headquartered in London and focused on promoting sustainable development and climate change adaptation worldwide. The organization works with communities, businesses, and institutional partners to support environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To promote climate action through community outreach, partnerships, and innovative environmental programs that improve livelihoods and protect natural resources.

Our Vision

A sustainable world where communities actively participate in environmental conservation and climate resilience.

Core Objectives

  • Support climate mitigation and adaptation programs.
  • Mobilize donors and institutional investors.
  • Promote environmental awareness.
  • Strengthen community partnerships.
